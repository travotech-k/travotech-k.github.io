At Travo Tech, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our mobile application, Explore France. Please review it carefully.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Details: Name, email, password, profile image (optional).
  • Location: Real-time location data to show nearby attractions, posts, and events.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your user profile.
  • To display relevant nearby posts, locations, and discussions.
  • To send app updates, notify users, and provide customer support.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Firebase for backend services. Your personal data is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising.

4. Location Data

  • Used only to suggest nearby experiences and posts.
  • Not stored or shared—processed in real-time only.
  • You can turn off location access via your device settings, but this may affect app features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal data with third parties unless legally required. All user information is kept confidential and used strictly within the scope of the app’s functionality.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is encrypted and securely stored using Firebase Security. We follow best practices to prevent unauthorized access or breaches.

7. User Rights

  • Delete Account: You can delete your account anytime, and your data will be permanently erased.
  • Edit Profile: Name and profile image can be updated within the app.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies or third-party trackers in the app.
